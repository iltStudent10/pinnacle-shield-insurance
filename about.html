About Pinnacle Shield Insurance

Founded in 1985 and headquartered in Hartford, Connecticut, Pinnacle Shield Insurance has spent decades helping families and businesses protect what matters most. What began as a regional provider has grown into a trusted name serving customers in all 50 states.

Our mission is simple: make insurance understandable, affordable, and dependable. We combine experienced advisors, transparent pricing, and digital tools that make it easier to compare options, customize coverage, and get support quickly.

Whether you need auto, home, or life insurance, our team is focused on long-term relationships, not one-time transactions. We are proud to deliver protection that adapts as your life changes.
